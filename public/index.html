Our Activities

Quick Dial, Inc started business long back in 2003. Over years we provided high quality services to our retail and wholesale clients. We built strong business network, that include many Tier1 companies. Our headquarter located in Brooklyn New York and another branch in Odessa, Ukraine. We always open for new business opportunities and will be happy to make your life and business better!

Exceptional customer approach

Our 24/7 routes monitoring and supervision makes an exceptional service for our clients. We offer cost-effective A-Z termination with exceptional quality.\

Reliable, and most importantly, quality equipment

Our equipment supports widely used industry standards including SIP, H.323 and performs codec conversion (G.729 (plain, A,B, AB), G.723.1, G.711, GSM FR, Speex, iLBC), which guarantees excellent quality of service and seamless connectivity between VoIP and TDM networks. We also have point code of FCC to work with TDM networks.

Help in choosing

We can help you to choose the most cost-effective solution based on your quality requirements.
